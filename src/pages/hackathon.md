In addition to the Arches developer meeting, Knowledge Integration are also hosting an extension to the meeting to give developers additional time to engage in a hackathon and to discuss any issues, features and follow-ups from the conference. 

This will be hosted at the **Kelham Island Museum** on the 11th June, and at the **Knowledge Integration offices** for the morning of the 12th June.

Please reach out to let us know if you are interested in attending!
