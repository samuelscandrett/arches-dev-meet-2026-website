**Knowledge Integration are delighted to welcome you to Sheffield, the home for the 2026 Arches Developer Meeting!**

<br>

## The 2026 location, Sheffield

The venue for this year is the [Millennium Gallery](https://www.sheffieldmuseums.org.uk/visit-us/millennium-gallery), home of Sheffield’s unique Metalwork Collection and Ruskin Collection of art and artefacts, and host to various major temporary exhibitions of art, craft, fashion and design. For information on travelling to the Millennium Gallery, visit our [venue](/venue) page.

If you're interested in learning more about to the sites and history of Sheffield, or just keen to find out where the nearest coffee shop or pub is, head over to our [about Sheffield](/about-sheffield) page on the home of the 2026 developer meeting that is Sheffield!

<br>

## Tickets

Registration for the Arches Developer Meeting 2026 is now open!

Tickets can be acquired via our [Eventbrite page](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/arches-developer-meeting-2026-tickets-1986440266310). The Arches Developer Meeting is a free event, though we ask attendees to sign up for tickets, as this will provide us with accurate figures on attendance and help with tasks such as room layout and catering.

<br>

## Event structure

The event is an in-person meeting only, **not hybrid**.

The event is hosted at the Millennium Gallery and will begin on **June 8th** at **12:00** and conclude on **June 10th** at **16:00**.

The formal [agenda](/agenda) is still being finalised using submissions from the call for proposals form, and topics that have been raised separately. Once the deadline has passed, we will produce the final agenda.

While we don't yet have the final agenda, we are expecting talks on Arches 8.1 and Lingo 1.0, and discussion topics including Arches core applications/modules and the roadmap.

Knowledge Integration are also hosting an extension to the meeting to give developers additional time to engage in a hackathon and to discuss any issues, features and follow-ups from the conference. This will be held at the **Kelham Island Museum** on the **11th**, and at the **Knowledge Integration offices** for the morning of the **12th**. See our [hackathon](/hackathon) page for more information.
