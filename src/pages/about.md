<br>

## The 2026 location, Sheffield

The venue for this year is the [Millennium Gallery](https://www.sheffieldmuseums.org.uk/visit-us/millennium-gallery), home of Sheffield’s unique Metalwork Collection and Ruskin Collection of art and artefacts, and host to various major temporary exhibitions of art, craft, fashion and design. For information on travelling to the Millennium Gallery, visit our page on the [venue](/venue).

If you're interested in learning more about to the sites and history of Sheffield, or just keen to find out where the nearest coffee shop or pub is, head over to our [page](/about-sheffield) on the home of the 2026 developer meeting that is Sheffield!

## Tickets

Registration for the Arches Developer Meeting 2026 is now open!

Tickets can be acquired via our [Eventbrite page](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/arches-developer-meeting-2026-tickets-1986440266310).
