**Please note the agenda is still being finalised.**

If you are interested in presenting Arches work/developments, or want to lead or suggest a group discussion topic, please do so by using our [call for proposals Google form](https://forms.gle/YQHiwSraLs1pSfi99).

The call for proposals has a deadline of **10/04**, so even if you’re unsure about the topic or your availability, please let us know what you’re thinking as soon as possible!

While we don't yet have the final agenda, we are expecting talks on Arches 8.1 and Lingo 1.0, and discussion topics including Arches core applications/modules and the roadmap.
