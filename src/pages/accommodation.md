## Hotels

Sheffield city centre has a wide range of hotels. Some hotels we may suggest are listed below. Note that Sheffield city centre is fairly compact, and none of the hotels listed are more than a 10 minute walk from the Millennium Gallery; this can be seen on the interactive map below.

- [Leopold Hotel Sheffield](https://www.leopoldhotel.co.uk/en/)
- [Raddison Blu Sheffield](https://www.radissonhotels.com/en-us/hotels/radisson-blu-sheffield)
- [Mercure Sheffield St Paul's Hotel & Spa](https://all.accor.com/booking/en/mercure/hotel/6628)
- [Novotel Sheffield Central](https://all.accor.com/booking/en/novotel/hotel/1348)
- [Premier Inn Sheffield City Centre (Angel Street)](https://www.premierinn.com/gb/en/hotels/england/south-yorkshire/sheffield/sheffield-city-centre-angel-street.html)
- [Travelodge Sheffield Central](https://www.travelodge.co.uk/hotels/317/Sheffield-Central-hotel)

<br>

All hotels listed are popular chains, with the exception of the Leopold Hotel, which is a hotel unique to Sheffield and filled with history.
